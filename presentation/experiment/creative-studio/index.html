What We Treat

Every treatment plan starts with your goals — not a one-size-fits-all protocol.

Sports Injuries

Torn ligament, pulled muscle, or nagging knee pain. Get an accurate diagnosis and a clear plan to return to the activities you love.

Joint Replacement

When pain limits your daily life, a new hip or knee can restore it. Modern techniques mean less pain and faster recovery.

Fracture Care

Broken bones need the right treatment from the start. Walk-in appointments available for urgent fractures and dislocations.

General Orthopaedics

Arthritis, tendon problems, carpal tunnel, back pain. If it involves bones, joints, or muscles, this is the place to start.
