# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Mission
Restore movement and quality of life through expert, compassionate orthopaedic care — close to home.

### Vision
Be the most trusted orthopaedic practice in the community, known for clinical excellence and genuine patient relationships.

### Values
- **Expertise** — Fellowship-trained, evidence-based care
- **Compassion** — Every patient is a person, not a case number
- **Clarity** — Honest communication, no jargon, transparent pricing
- **Accessibility** — Timely appointments, convenient location, responsive team

## Brand Voice

| Attribute     | We are...                        | We are NOT...                  |
|---------------|----------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Tone          | Warm, confident, reassuring      | Cold, clinical, intimidating   |
| Language      | Plain English, clear explanations| Heavy medical jargon           |
| Authority     | Knowledgeable, experienced       | Arrogant, dismissive           |
| Personality   | Approachable, human              | Corporate, impersonal          |

### Writing Examples

**Good:** "A torn ACL doesn't have to sideline you for good. Let's talk about your options."
**Bad:** "Anterior cruciate ligament rupture necessitates surgical intervention consultation."

**Good:** "Most patients are walking the same day after knee replacement."
**Bad:** "Post-operative ambulation is typically achieved within 24 hours of total knee arthroplasty."

## Visual Direction

### Colour Palette
- **Primary:** Deep blue — trust, professionalism, calm
- **Secondary:** White/light grey — cleanliness, clarity
- **Accent:** Teal or green — health, vitality, recovery

### Typography
- Headlines: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Helvetica Neue)
- Body: Highly readable sans-serif at comfortable sizes

### Imagery
- Real patient interactions (with consent), not generic stock photos
- Active people of diverse ages — walking, playing sport, enjoying life
- Bright, clean clinical environments
- Avoid: overly graphic surgical imagery in patient-facing materials

### Logo Usage
- Always maintain clear space around the logo
- Minimum size: 32px height for digital, 12mm for print
- Do not stretch, rotate, or alter colours
