# Product & Services

## Practice Overview

Local orthopaedic surgery practice providing diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation for musculoskeletal conditions — bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

## Core Services

### Consultations
- Initial orthopaedic assessment and diagnosis
- Second opinion consultations
- Pre-operative evaluations
- Post-operative follow-ups

### Non-Surgical Treatments
- Joint injections (corticosteroid, hyaluronic acid, PRP)
- Casting and splinting for fractures
- Physical therapy referrals and coordination
- Pain management plans
- Bracing and orthotics

### Surgical Procedures
- **Joint Replacement** — hip, knee, and shoulder arthroplasty
- **Arthroscopy** — minimally invasive joint surgery (knee, shoulder, ankle)
- **Fracture Repair** — open reduction and internal fixation (ORIF)
- **Ligament Reconstruction** — ACL, rotator cuff repair
- **Spinal Surgery** — discectomy, laminectomy, spinal fusion
- **Hand & Wrist Surgery** — carpal tunnel release, tendon repair

### Sports Medicine
- Athletic injury assessment and treatment
- Return-to-play evaluations
- Concussion management referrals
- Performance optimisation guidance

## Service Delivery Model

| Channel        | Description                              |
|----------------|------------------------------------------|
| In-clinic      | Primary consultations and minor procedures |
| Hospital/ASC   | Surgical procedures under anaesthesia     |
| Telehealth     | Follow-up appointments and triage         |
| Urgent walk-in | Acute fractures and injuries              |

## Key Differentiators

- Fellowship-trained orthopaedic surgeon with sub-specialty expertise
- Same-week appointments for acute injuries
- End-to-end care from diagnosis through rehabilitation
- Transparent pricing for self-pay patients
- Modern imaging (X-ray, MRI referral) available on-site or coordinated
